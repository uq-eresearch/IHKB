People

Carroll Go-Sam

Discovery Indigenous Award Chief Investigator

Carroll Go-Sam is a researcher and manager of AERC at the University of Queensland’s School of Architecture. Carroll is a descendant of the Dyirrbal people of the upper Tully and upper Herbert Rivers in far north Queensland.

c.gosam@uq.edu.au

Mark Moran

Chief Investigator

Mark Moran is a Professor in Development Effectiveness at the University of Queensland's Institute for Social Science Research. He is closely affiliated with the Aboriginal Environment Research Centre.

mark.moran@uq.edu.au

Paul Memmott

Chief Investigator

Professor Paul Memmott is an anthropologist and architect and is the Director of the Aboriginal Environments Research Centre (AERC) at the University of Queensland (Institute for Social Science Research and School of Architecture).

p.memmott@uq.edu.au

Jane Hunter

Chief Investigator

Jane Hunter is Professor of eResearch at the University of Queensland’s School of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering, where she leads the eResearch Group.

j.hunter@uq.edu.au
